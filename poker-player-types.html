Slow Player
🕷️
Trapper
The Slow Player

Checks disguise monster hands. Hits a set on the turn? Checks. Flops the nuts? Checks. Lures opponents into betting their whole stack into a dead pot. Patient, deceptive, and lethal when the trap springs.

Deceptive Patient Check-Raise Traps Set Mining
⚡ How to exploit
When they check back on a dry board, check behind — don't bluff into them. Be cautious when they wake up with sudden aggression. Their bet on the river often means the nuts.
⚠️ Their Weakness
By checking strong hands, they give free cards to flush/straight draws. They miss value from calling hands. A disciplined TAG won't pay them off on the river.
